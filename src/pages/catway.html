What is a "cat"?

A cat is like a tiny, furry dictator who demands respect when it’s convenient for them and ignores you when it’s not. They’ll act like royalty, lounging around in random spots, and when they’re awake, they’re either a ninja, suddenly darting around with incredible agility, or a clumsy mess tripping over nothing. They’re expert nap-takers, making it look like an art form, and they’re always ready to knock something off the counter just to remind you who’s in charge. Affectionate but distant, playful but lazy — cats are full of contradictions, yet somehow, they’re the adorable overlords of the house.
